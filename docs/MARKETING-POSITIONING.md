# Marketing positioning — WhatsApp AI Desk

Internal reference for copy, ads, and future pages. Phase 1 implemented in marketing site (Jan 2026).

## Category

**Own:** Local desktop AI desk for business WhatsApp  
**Do not compete in:** Cloud WhatsApp chatbot / team inbox SaaS

## One-line positioning

> AI inbox for WhatsApp — on your PC, with your key, under your control.

## Three pillars

| Pillar | Message |
|--------|---------|
| Local-first | WhatsApp session + keys stay on your PC |
| BYOK | OpenRouter key; AI bills to customer, no markup |
| Human-in-the-loop | Draft queue — AI suggests, you approve before send |

## Primary ICP

Solo operators and small teams (1–3 people) running sales/support on WhatsApp who want AI help without:

- Meta API onboarding
- Cloud inbox lock-in
- Hidden AI fees

**Strong verticals:** freelancers/agencies, local services, privacy-sensitive operators, emerging markets (EN/Urdu/Hindi/Arabic/ES in-app).

## Honest filter (say this out loud)

Not for you if you need:

- Multi-agent team inbox + CRM
- Official Meta API at enterprise scale
- Fully autonomous bots

## Pricing story

- **We sell:** software license + support
- **Customer pays separately:** OpenRouter usage (BYOK)
- **Hook:** $29/mo Pro + ~$5–20 OpenRouter vs $49–99+/mo cloud SaaS + bundled AI

**Lifetime $499:** agencies standardizing one Windows desk.

## Messaging do / don't

**Do:**

- "Desktop copilot" / "approve before send"
- Transparent BYOK costs
- Five-minute QR connect
- Dedicated business number (acceptable use)

**Don't:**

- "Enterprise WhatsApp API"
- "Fully autonomous AI agent"
- "Team inbox / CRM" (not yet)
- Hide unofficial WhatsApp tradeoff
